How to Reach Us

The nearest Airport to Naukuchia Tal is Pant Nagar Airport situated 74kms from Naukuchia Tal. If you're travelling by train Kathgodam is the nearest railway station which is 44kms from Naukuchia Tal.

Naukuchia Tal is well connected by roads connecting major cities and towns of Uttarakhand. Buses and taxis are easily available from Almora, Nainital and Kathdodam to Naukuchiatal.
